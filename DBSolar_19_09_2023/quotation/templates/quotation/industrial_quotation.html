3. SCOPE OF WORK (By HERAMB INDUSTRIES) :

Our comprehensive turnkey solution includes:

Design & Engineering:

  • Detailed Site Survey & Feasibility Study.
  • System Sizing & Layout Design (including shadow analysis).
  • Electrical SLD (Single Line Diagram) preparation.
  • Structural analysis of the rooftop (if required, third-party/Paid service).
  • Submission of detailed engineering drawings for approval.

Procurement & Supply:

  • High-efficiency Solar PV Modules.
  • Grid-Tied String Inverters.
  • Robust Mounting Structure (Aluminum/Galvanized Iron).
  • DC & AC Cables, Conduits, and Trays.
  • DC & AC Distribution Boards, String Combiner Boxes.
  • Earthing System.
  • Monitoring System (remote monitoring unit).
  • All necessary hardware, connectors (MC4), and safety components.

Installation & Commissioning:

  • Foundation and mounting structure installation.
  • PV Module installation and string wiring.
  • Inverter installation and electrical connections.
  • Installation of AC/DC distribution boards and protective devices.
  • Installation of earthing.
  • Cabling, cable management, and trenching (if applicable).
  • Comprehensive testing of the entire system.
  • Final commissioning and handover.

Liaison & Approvals:

  • Assistance with MSEDCL/CEIG approval processes for net metering.
  • All necessary documentation for project completion and approvals.

Training & Documentation:

  • Operational training for your designated personnel.
  • Provision of user manuals, warranties, and as-built drawings.
